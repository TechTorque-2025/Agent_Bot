TECHTORQUE AUTO SERVICES - HOW TO BOOK AN APPOINTMENT

BOOKING YOUR SERVICE APPOINTMENT IS EASY!

ONLINE BOOKING (RECOMMENDED):
1. Log in to your TechTorque account at our website
2. Navigate to your Customer Dashboard
3. Click on "Book Appointment" or "Schedule Service"
4. Select your vehicle from your registered vehicles
5. Choose the type of service you need
6. Pick your preferred date and time slot
7. Add any special notes or concerns
8. Confirm your appointment
9. You'll receive a confirmation email and SMS

THROUGH THE CHATBOT:
You can ask me (the AI Assistant) to help you check available appointment slots!
Just say something like:
- "I want to book an appointment"
- "Show me available time slots"
- "When can I schedule service?"
- "Check appointment availability"

WALK-IN SERVICE:
While we accept walk-ins, appointments are highly recommended to:
- Guarantee service on your preferred date
- Reduce wait times
- Ensure parts availability
- Get priority service

APPOINTMENT REQUIREMENTS:
- Valid account with TechTorque
- Vehicle must be registered in your account
- Service type selection
- Preferred date and time
- Contact information (phone and email)

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER BOOKING:
1. Confirmation notification sent immediately
2. Reminder sent 24 hours before appointment
3. Service advisor assigned to your case
4. Estimated service time provided
5. Cost estimate shared (if applicable)

CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Free cancellation up to 24 hours before appointment
- Please notify us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule
- You can modify your appointment through your dashboard

SAME-DAY APPOINTMENTS:
Available based on technician availability and service type
- Call us for same-day emergency services
- Some services may require advance scheduling

For the best experience, we recommend booking 2-3 days in advance!
